DungeonScribe / Project Overview

Why we made it, who it‚Äôs for, and how it helps DMs run unforgettable sessions.

Purpose

Reduce cognitive load for DMs by centralising notes, timelines, and NPCs with a tactile, moody UI.

Audience

Tabletop groups who value immersion and flow; DMs who need fast recall and lightweight structure.

Design

Dark-fantasy parchment, cursor-tracked lighting, warm gold accents, soft motion, and clean type.

Outcomes

Quicker session prep, smoother improvisation, and better continuity across long-running campaigns.

How to Use

  1. Choose a campaign from the carousel.
  2. Use the side panels to manage notes, NPCs, and session flow.
  3. Toggle themes and light mode to suit your table.
  4. Use keyboard arrows to glide the campaign cards.
